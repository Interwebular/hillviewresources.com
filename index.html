Kal Minerals Starts Portfolio of Aluminium-Supporting Mineral Properties - March 10, 2016

We are pleased to announce that we have the option to earn a 100% interest in the Jack White Property in the New Westminster Mining Division in British Columbia, Canada.

The Jack White property is a Skarn-type mineral deposit in the heart of a regional Scandium anomaly. The property is also prospective for copper, silver, gold, tungsten, and molybdenum. We are in the process of planning a development plan for this property.

Scandium is used in aluminium alloys and thus supports our vision of supporting the aluminium industry.
